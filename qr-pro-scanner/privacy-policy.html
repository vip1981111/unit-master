Privacy Policy

QR Pro Scanner

Last Updated: January 25, 2026
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how QR Pro Scanner collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

QR Pro Scanner is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Camera Access: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes. Images are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server.
  • Scan History: Stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your scan history.
  • Purchase Information: Processed securely by Apple. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide QR code and barcode scanning functionality
  • Save your scan history locally for your convenience
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple's secure payment system
  • Display relevant advertisements (free version only)

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Scan history is saved in local storage
  • App preferences are stored locally
  • No data is uploaded to external servers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases securely.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your scan history at any time within the app
  • Disable camera access through your device settings
  • Request information about data we may have

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data security.
